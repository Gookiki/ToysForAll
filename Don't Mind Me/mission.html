Our Mission

Creating opportunities and inclusive play for children across our communities.

Equal Opportunity

We provide equal opportunity and enrichment to students around the community, particularly for those in disabled positions. Our volunteers and partners help design and deliver tailored toys and resources.

Inclusive Design

Our design process centers on accessibility and collaboration with therapists, educators, and families to deliver meaningful, usable toys.

Get Involved

Volunteer, donate, or partner with us to help bring adaptive toys to children who need them most.
